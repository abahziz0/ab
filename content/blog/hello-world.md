I've been meaning to write more for a while. Here's why I'm finally doing it.

## Why a blog in 2024?

Short answer: I need somewhere to think out loud. I spend a lot of time reading other people's writing — essays, changelogs, post-mortems, research notes — and the best ones always feel like someone just sat down and worked through something they didn't fully understand yet.

That's what I want this to be. Not a tutorial site, not a portfolio-as-content-marketing machine. Just a place to write things down before I forget them.

## What to expect

A mix of things:

- **Code and tooling** — what I'm building, what broke, what I'd do differently
- **Design notes** — since I care about interfaces and the gap between how things look and how they actually work
- **Reading lists** — books, papers, links worth saving
- **Occasional long-form** — when something deserves more than a bullet point

Posts will be irregular. I'm not optimising for consistency here.

## One rule

No takes I wouldn't say out loud. If I wouldn't explain it to a colleague over coffee, it shouldn't be a post.
